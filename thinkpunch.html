Getting Started

Welcome to ThinkPunch! Our app is designed to help you time and score your boxing chess battles with ease.

Quick Start Guide:

  • Open the app and tap on the "Battle" tab
  • Select your preferred number of rounds (1, 3, or 5)
  • Choose round duration (1, 3, or 5 minutes)
  • Tap "Start Battle" to begin
  • Use the +/- buttons to track scores during each round

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track scores during a battle?

During each round, you'll see score counters for both players. Simply tap the + button to add a point or the - button to subtract a point for each player.

Can I pause a battle?

Yes, you can end a round early by tapping the "End Round" button. The app will automatically save your progress.

How long are the rest periods between rounds?

Rest periods are 60 seconds by default. You can skip the rest period by tapping the "Skip Rest" button.

Where is my battle history stored?

All your battle data is stored locally on your device. You can view past battles in the "Records" tab.

Privacy & Data

ThinkPunch respects your privacy. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information or battle data.
